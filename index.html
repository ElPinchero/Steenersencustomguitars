A Lifetime of Craftsmanship

Every Steenersen guitar is handcrafted by Pål Steenersen in Norway. Drawing on more than three decades of experience building award-winning custom motorcycles, each instrument reflects meticulous craftsmanship, carefully selected tonewoods, premium hardware and an uncompromising attention to detail. Only a handful of guitars leave the workshop each year.

Featured Build

Every instrument is built from scratch using carefully selected materials and traditional methods combined with modern precision.

Meet the Builder

My name is Pål Steenersen. I have spent over thirty years creating award-winning custom motorcycles, fabricating everything from fuel tanks and fenders to machining, custom paint, leatherwork and pinstriping. Music has always been a part of my life. After years of modifying and refinishing guitars, I built my first instrument entirely from scratch in 2019. Today I build only a small number of guitars each year, allowing every instrument to receive the time, care and attention it deserves.
