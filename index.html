Meet the Builder

My name is Pål Steenersen. I have spent more than 30 years building award-winning custom motorcycles, creating everything from sheet metal fabrication and machining to paintwork, leathercraft and pinstriping. Music has always been a part of my life, and after years of modifying and refinishing guitars I built my first instrument completely from scratch in 2019. Today every Steenersen guitar is handcrafted in Norway using carefully selected tonewoods, premium hardware and techniques developed through a lifetime of craftsmanship. Only a handful of guitars leave the workshop each year.
