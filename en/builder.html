The Builder

The story behind Steenersen Custom Guitars.

Pål Steenersen

Born in 1971, I have spent my entire life creating things with my hands. Music has always been an important part of my life, and during my younger years I played drums, guitar and mandolin in several bands.

For more than thirty years I have designed and built award-winning custom motorcycles, fabricating fuel tanks, fenders, machined components, custom paintwork, leatherwork and hand-painted pinstriping.

My fascination with guitars eventually led me from refinishing and modifying instruments to building them completely from scratch. In 2019 I completed my very first handcrafted guitar.

Since then I have developed my own jigs, fixtures and workshop tools, allowing every instrument to be built with exceptional precision and attention to detail.

Only a handful of guitars leave the workshop each year. Every instrument is individually handcrafted with no compromises in quality, tone or finish.

My Philosophy

I believe the finest instruments are created when quality is placed above quantity. Every guitar is built one at a time using carefully selected tonewoods, premium hardware and traditional craftsmanship combined with modern precision.

My goal is simple: To build instruments that inspire musicians today and remain treasured for generations.
