The Workshop

Every Steenersen guitar begins as carefully selected timber and is built completely by hand.

From Raw Wood to Finished Instrument

The workshop has been built around precision, craftsmanship and traditional techniques. Many of the jigs, fixtures and specialty tools used in the process have been designed and made specifically for building these guitars.

Custom Paint & Details

My background in award-winning custom motorcycles allows me to incorporate unique finishes including metallic flake, candy colours, pinstriping and other custom paint techniques into selected guitar builds.

No Mass Production

Only a handful of instruments leave the workshop every year. Every guitar is built individually with patience, precision and uncompromising attention to detail.
