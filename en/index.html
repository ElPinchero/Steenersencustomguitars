A Lifetime of Craftsmanship

Every Steenersen guitar is handcrafted by Pål Steenersen in Norway. Drawing on more than three decades of experience building award-winning custom motorcycles, each instrument reflects meticulous craftsmanship, carefully selected tonewoods, premium hardware and uncompromising attention to detail.

Only a handful of guitars leave the workshop each year. Every instrument receives the time, care and precision it deserves.
