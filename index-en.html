A Lifetime of Craftsmanship

Every Steenersen guitar is handcrafted by Pål Steenersen in Norway. Drawing on more than three decades of experience building award-winning custom motorcycles, every instrument reflects meticulous craftsmanship, carefully selected tonewoods, premium hardware and an uncompromising attention to detail. Only a handful of guitars leave the workshop each year, allowing every build to receive the time and care it deserves.
