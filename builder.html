Meet the Builder

Pål Steenersen

Born in 1971, I have been passionate about music, craftsmanship and custom building for as long as I can remember. Throughout my youth I played drums, guitar and mandolin in several bands while spending every spare moment creating and building things.

For more than thirty years I have built award-winning custom motorcycles from the ground up, producing everything from sheet metal fabrication and machining to paintwork, leatherwork and custom pinstriping. That experience taught me precision, patience and attention to detail—qualities that naturally carried over into guitar building.

After years of modifying and refinishing my own instruments, I built my first guitar completely from scratch in 2019. Since then I have designed and built my own jigs, templates and specialised tools, allowing every instrument to be handcrafted in my own workshop.

Only a handful of guitars leave the workshop each year. Every guitar is individually crafted using carefully selected tonewoods, premium hardware and traditional craftsmanship combined with decades of experience in custom fabrication and finishing.

My goal has never been mass production. It is simply to build the finest guitars I possibly can.
